Our Services

Wintech Engineering

"The most versatile machine for cutting flexible foam"

Custom Contours

Blades on both axes provides the ultimate control to contour cutting. The horizontal blade can cut shapes while the vertical blade can cut them to length — all in one set-up without the operator needed throughout the process.

Peeling

Turn a block of foam into a single long sheet with a "peel" or spiral cut. Peel any shape — circle, square and even oblong — with minimal product waste. This is included as a standard cut in the software; simply enter the desired peel thickness.

Sheeting

The blade can cut on both passes, allowing a block of foam to be quickly sliced into sheets of equal or varying thickness. The software also compensates for the thickness of the blade, so all cuts are done with incredible accuracy.
