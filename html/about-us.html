We are

Dyman Foams

"The foundation of our Company is our people. We are proud of our Company, our products and our achievements."

At Dyman, we believe there is a better way to do... everthing

A more valuable, less invasive way where customers are earned rather than bought. We're obsessively passionate about it, and our mission is to help people receive exactly what they want. We focus on quality > quantity. It's one of the least understood and least transparent aspects of a great company, and we see that as an opportunity: We're excited to simplify the Foam industry.

Dyman Foams is a privately owned Queensland born and bred Company established in 1992.
All current operations are undertaken from our 6000 M2 building at 105 Robinson Road Geebung Brisbane where we proudly employ some 50 Queenslanders. Due to the diversity of our markets and products Dyman Foams also trades under the names Uptown Furniture and Factory Direct Furniture. The primary business of Dyman Foams is foam conversion to the Domestic and Commercial Furniture Industry, to the Bedding Industry, Transport Industry, Sporting and Leisure Industry, Packaging Industry as well as to the Mining and Construction Industries. When this broad spread of product is coupled with state of the art computerised and manual cutting equipment operated by the most experienced staff in the Industry, Dyman Foams can proudly say we are the front runners in foam conversion in Queensland and have been for over a decade. This is no idle boast - it is a fact.

In 1995 Dyman Foams made a decision to vertically integrate its supply chain and began manufacturing upholstered furniture for the local domestic and commercial markets. Some 20 years later we are now one of the leading Furniture Manufacturers in Australia trading under the name Uptown Furniture. Uptown Furniture manufactures a variety of upholstered furniture including chairs, sofas and sofa beds, as well as corner suites and chaise lounges. Uptown Furniture can be further researched at uptownfurniture.com.au

In 2009 Dyman Foams, due to the lack of support for local manufacturers by retailers in general, decided to again vertically integrate its supply chain further and, as a consequence, started retailing under the Factory Direct Furniture banner. This outlet sells all products manufactured by Uptown Furniture as well as, where possible, supporting other local manufacturers. Factory Direct Furniture can be further researched at factorydirectfurniture.com.au

The founding Managing Director of Dyman Foams, Kevin Feldman, has been a long time servant of the Furniture Industry Association of Queensland, including 6 years as President and was, in 2009 granted Life Membership. In that time he has also served for over 10 years on the National Body. Dyman Foams is a respected Queensland Company, employing Queenslanders and making quality products for the local market. At the same time Dyman Foams has never lost sight of its responsibility to the Industry nor to the community and this has been demonstrated over time.
